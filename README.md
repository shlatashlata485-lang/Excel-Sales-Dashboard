☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard
Project Overview
Developed an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze coffee shop sales data and transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard provides management with a clear view of sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product popularity, payment preferences, and operational efficiency.
Business Problem
The coffee shop generated a large amount of sales data every day, making it difficult for managers to quickly answer questions such as:
Which products sell the most?
Which days are the busiest?
Which shift generates the highest number of orders?
How do customers prefer to pay?
How are sales changing over time?
Instead of reviewing raw spreadsheets, management needed a simple dashboard to support data-driven decision making.
MySolution
I built an interactive Excel dashboard using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and KPI cards that summarizes business performance into one screen.
The dashboard includes:
Total Sales
Total Orders
Total Products
Best Selling Coffee
Orders by Month
Orders by Day
Orders by Shift
Payment Method Analysis
Key Business Insights
Revenue Performance
Total Sales reached $42,627.58.
The dashboard allows management to monitor overall business performance instantly.
Customer Demand
A total of 1,313 customer orders were analyzed.
Sales were distributed across 8 coffee products.
Product Performance
The dashboard identified the best-selling drinks.
Top performers include:
Americano With Milk (319 orders)
Latte (282 orders)
Cappuccino (222 orders)
Low-performing products include:
Cocoa (44 orders)
Espresso (59 orders)
Payment Analysis
Customer payment behavior showed:
93.2% Card Payments
6.8% Cash Payments
This insight helps management prioritize digital payment services and reduce cash handling.
Weekly Sales Pattern
The dashboard identifies demand across weekdays.
Highest demand:
Tuesday
Thursday
Lowest demand:
Sunday
This helps managers schedule employees more efficiently.
Monthly Performance
Sales trends reveal:
Peak month: May (267 orders)
Lowest month: August (180 orders)

Management can investigate seasonal demand and prepare marketing campaigns during weaker months.
Shift Analysis
Orders by shift:
Evening: 469 orders
Afternoon: 435 orders
Morning: 409 orders
The dashboard shows that the evening shift is the busiest, helping optimize staff allocation.
Business Impact
This dashboard enables business owners to:
Monitor business performance in real time.
Identify best-selling and low-performing products.
Improve inventory planning.
Optimize employee scheduling.
Understand customer payment behavior.
Monitor monthly trends.
Reduce manual reporting time.
Make faster, data-driven business decisions.
